## Situation when received project

- Unstable ranking for strategic keyword “tóc giả nam” => low content entity
- Traffic drops after other channels, campaign ends => unstable SEO performance
- Traffic mostly come from REALLY TOP funnel regarding “kiểu tóc nam” => low potential to convert leads

---

## Scope of work

- Lead Search strategy (SEO, AEO, GEO Local Maps, PR, Social) & Google Ads and direct cross-functional team to drive website growth focusing on GMV & CR improvements.
- Collaborate with development, sales, and content teams to improve website sales journey.
- Built a sustainable growth model through Search strategy, CRO, Google Ads, and website touchpoints optimization.

---

## KPIs

- SEO = Ranking, Organic traffic and Conversion Rate (Form submit, CTA button clicks)
- Paid Ads = Business metrics (Revenue, CIR), Campaign metrics (Spending, CPA, CAC)

---

## Achievement

- Increased website leads by +134%, peaking at +222% with average CR (Sessions to Leads) around avg. 10%.
- Boosted website-driven revenue: starting from Apr 2024 as the 100% baseline, reached a peak at +270% — equal to 2.4x growth within 15 months, +6% MoM

---

## Strategy

- Re-organize website components, pages, segments with UIUX proposal & website audit
- Propose keyword strategy and content direction
- Manage team members in SEO & Google Ads execution
