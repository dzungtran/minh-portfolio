## Situation when received project

- Organic traffic = 21k/ month
- 50K SV top 10 within industry keyword bank in Category Page Type
- 130k SV Top 5 in Blog Page Type
- During migration process

---

## Achievement

- Organic traffic = 40k/ month, GROWTH RATE = **+91%**
- Push 200K SV to top 10 in Category Page Type, GROWTH RATE = **+400%**
- Push 180k SV Top 5 in Blog Page Type, GROWTH RATE = **+40%*
- Only losing traffic -10% during migration

---

## Strategy

- Footer Text for Category Pages
- Blog by Keyword Bank
- Migration checklist
